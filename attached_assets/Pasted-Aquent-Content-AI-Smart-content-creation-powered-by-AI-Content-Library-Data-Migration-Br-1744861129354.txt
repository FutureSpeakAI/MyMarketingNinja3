Creative Brief for COPD Treatment X Emails

1. Project Overview

        Project Name: COPD Treatment X
        Project Description: Develop three rep-triggered emails promoting our new COPD treatment. These emails will be sent following representative visits to healthcare providers, offering three levels of interaction based on provider engagement.

        Background/Context: The emails are intended to reinforce the information shared during in-person visits and encourage healthcare providers to prescribe our new COPD treatment. The emails will provide additional insights and resources to support their decision-making process.

2. Objectives

• Encourage healthcare providers to prescribe the new COPD treatment.

• Reinforce the safety and popularity of the treatment.

• Increase awareness and familiarity with the treatment among providers.

3. Target Audience

        Primary Audience: Healthcare providers who see COPD patients.

• Role: Physicians, pulmonologists, and healthcare specialists dealing with COPD patients.

• Needs: Reliable, evidence-based treatment options for COPD.

• Challenges: Managing patient outcomes while ensuring treatment safety and efficacy.

4. Key Messages

• Our COPD treatment is exceptionally safe and well-received by the medical community.

• Evidence-based studies demonstrate the treatment's efficacy in improving patient outcomes.

• Healthcare providers can confidently prescribe our treatment, knowing it is backed by robust clinical data.

5. Deliverables

• Three separate emails, each tailored to a different level of provider engagement:

• Email 1: Introduction and overview of the COPD treatment.

• Email 2: In-depth analysis of treatment safety and efficacy.

• Email 3: Case studies and testimonials from other healthcare providers.

6. Content Creation Guidelines

• Tone/Voice: Educational, authoritative, and supportive.

• Length: Each email should be medium-length, ranging from 500 to 1000 words.

• Terminology: Use medical terminology appropriately, ensuring clarity and understanding.

7. Timeline

• Start Date: Immediate

• Deadline: Two weeks from project start

• Milestones:

• Week 1: Drafts of all three emails completed.

• Week 2: Review, revisions, and final approval.

8. Content Creation Instructions

• Create an engaging subject line for each email that highlights the treatment's safety and popularity.

• Begin each email with a brief introduction that re-establishes the context of the representative's visit.

• For Email 1, provide a comprehensive overview of the COPD treatment, highlighting key benefits and features.

• For Email 2, dive deeper into the clinical safety data, providing evidence and studies that support the treatment's efficacy.

• For Email 3, incorporate real-world testimonials and case studies from other healthcare providers who have successfully prescribed the treatment.

• Include a call-to-action in each email, encouraging providers to contact their representative for more information or to initiate the prescription process.

• Ensure all content is proofread for accuracy, clarity, and grammatical correctness.
